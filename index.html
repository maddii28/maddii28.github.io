About Me

Hi, I'm Maddhav Suneja, a Computer Science major at New York University, with minors in Mathematics and Quantum Technology. I'm passionate about developing creative solutions to real-world challenges through technology. My work includes projects like Finlitify, a financial literacy platform that earned the Best WolframAlpha Hack Award at HackNYU 2023, and Lockabox, a smart lockbox incorporating text-recognition technology to balance focus and accessibility. Currently, I'm building QRaven, which leverages Flask and machine learning to generate dynamic QR codes with advanced analytics. I'm continuously honing my skills in machine learning, AI, and backend development, striving to create impactful, user-centered applications.
